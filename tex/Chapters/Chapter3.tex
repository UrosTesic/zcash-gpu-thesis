\section{zCash}

Cryptocurrencies have gained in popularity over the last couple of years. The best example is the Bitcoin bubble which made people gain millions over night, and lose them soon after. However, Bitcoin is of limited use today. It cannot be used as a general currency due to the low throughput and long waiting times for the transaction to be added to the block. Its status as an anonymous currency is also disputed, resulting in 2 different cryptocurrencies being developed to fill that void - Monero and zCash.\\
\\
ZCash defines two types of addresses. Transparent addresses behave the same way as Bitcoin addresses. Data resides in the public blockchain, so it can be tracked the same way as with Bitcoin. However, shielded addresses reveal nothing when they are a part of the transaction.
